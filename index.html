Accessle is a company that speeds up your digital transformation from (legacy) applications and to other applications. Without altering the source or target application.

From Windows to ESB

For this governmental customer Accessle unlocked a 25 year old windows application still in use. The target was their enterprise service bus (ESB). The connection was established in a day. By unlocking vital information of previous cliëntele these became accessable again for the business processes of this customer, without the tedious work of synchronizing by hand.

From SaaS to SaaS

This customer was confronted with SaaS use by a business unit that was not authorized by the IT department. The result was that the data was not integrated with other applications of the company. Accessle made a temporally solution that synchronized two applications. So the naughty department didn't lose time and the data was available for the other parts of the company. After a while the illegal SaaS was substituted with something integrated in the architecture.

From CMS to CMS

This case involves a city in Europe that used a CMS that was made twenty something years ago. The company that serviced it filed for bankruptcy. This left this city with no means to move to a more modern CMS. Accessle helpt them by getting the content and add it to a new CMS. After the migration, the old CMS could finally be decommisioned.

From Delphi to SaaS.

A customer made a Delphi application, but as time moved on this application was harder and harder to maintain. So it was decided to make a new more modern version. Accessle helped with migrating data from the old application to the new one. This meant that the customer's scarce project resources were available for building the new application.

From Java (J2EE) to new CMS

A Dutch municipality needed to have their data migrated from an old CMS system to a new one. This case mainly involved address data and a little bit of history. Accessle provided a means with less effort than building a export function in the old application. Saving time and money by facilitating a project that was completed in time and on budget.

From Mainframe to Webapp

For this governmental customer Accessle unlocked a 40 year old database still in use. The target application was build in an hour using AppSheet and the screens where read by artificial intelligence (AI) and migrated to the new application. The total savings the next year where more than €100000 in license fees, far less than this customer payed us.
